> Organize work. Collaborate better. Build faster.

SyncNode is a collaborative project management web application that helps teams keep everything in one place. Instead of switching between chats, notes, and spreadsheets, you can organize your work inside shared workspaces, create projects, manage tasks, and stay updated with your team.

Whether it's a college project, a startup idea, or a freelance team, SyncNode helps everyone stay on the same page.

---

## Why I Built SyncNode

Every team starts with a simple plan.

Then someone creates a WhatsApp group.

Someone else shares a Google Doc.

Tasks disappear in hundreds of messages.

Deadlines become "I thought it was next week."

And suddenly everyone is asking...

**"Wait... who's doing this task?"** 😅

I built SyncNode to make teamwork less confusing and more organized.

Everything lives in one place, so your team can spend more time building and less time searching.

---

## What You Can Do

- Create your own account
- Create workspaces for different teams
- Invite teammates
- Create projects
- Add and manage tasks
- Upload files for your tasks
- Track progress
- Collaborate with your team in real time

SyncNode is a full-stack web application built as a learning project to understand how modern collaboration tools work.

The goal was simple:

**Make teamwork feel less messy.**

If SyncNode saves your team from asking **"Who's working on this?"** even once, then it has already done its job. 😄
